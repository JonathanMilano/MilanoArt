Hello and welcome! I am Jonathan Milano

An artist based in the serene town of Boulder City, Nevada. With a deep passion for creativity, my artistic journey encompasses traditional art, digital and graphic design, and the dynamic music project, 'Static Oracle,' among other "side quests".

During my formative years, I experienced a diverse range of cultures and landscapes. Having lived in various parts of the United States and traversed through different corners of Europe, I gained a unique perspective that continuously influences my work. These diverse experiences have woven a rich tapestry of inspiration, allowing me to infuse a global essence into my art.

Beyond the canvas, my life finds harmony in the practice of Zen Buddhism. Rooted in mindfulness and meditation, this spiritual journey has deeply shaped both my personal and creative endeavors. The principles of Zen guide my artistic process, encouraging me to find beauty in simplicity and to embrace the present moment in all its raw authenticity.

Nourishing my soul in tandem with my creative aspirations, I adhere to a vegetarian diet. This conscious choice reflects my respect for life in all its forms and serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of everything around us. By adopting a vegetarian lifestyle, I strive to honor the environment and contribute positively to the world we share.

My work is a reflection of the experiences, values, and perspectives that have molded me into the artist I am today.
